The Short Version

If you skipped the quiz, read this.

Best Value Play

Osmo Pocket 3

Two and a half years on, this is still the gold standard for cinematic solo-creator video. Now that the Pocket 4 is out, Pocket 3 prices will soften, and for anyone on a budget who wants the gimbal magic without the 4K/240fps flex, this is the smart buy.

Best Pure Vlogging Tool

Osmo Pocket 4

If your primary job is shooting yourself or someone else walking, talking, and moving through a space, nothing touches it. 4K/240fps, 14 stops, 107GB internal, mechanical gimbal. The ultimate single-purpose tool. Not available in the US.

Best All-Round Device

iPhone 17 Pro Max

The only camera here that's also a phone, an editor, a streaming rig, and a business computer. ProRes RAW, Log 2, Genlock, three focal lengths. For client work and travel where you can't justify a second device, it wins on pure versatility.
